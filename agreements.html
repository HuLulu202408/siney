Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Siney collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when you use the Siney mobile application, websites, services, features, and related content.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly, information generated when you use the service, and information needed to keep the service safe and reliable.

  • Account information, such as nickname, profile information, login identifiers, authentication information, and settings.
  • User content, such as posts, comments, messages, profile details, images, reports, feedback, and other materials you create, upload, send, or receive.
  • Safety and moderation information, such as content reports, blocked account records, enforcement history, appeal records, and signals used to detect spam, fraud, harassment, sexual exploitation, hate, threats, violence, or other objectionable content.
  • Device and usage information, such as device model, operating system, app version, language, approximate region, IP address, crash logs, diagnostics, interaction logs, and security events.
  • Customer support information, such as messages you send to us, attachments, contact details, and issue history.

2. How We Use Information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, personalize, maintain, and improve Siney.
  • To create and manage accounts, authenticate users, sync preferences, and provide customer support.
  • To display, distribute, rank, recommend, and moderate user-generated content.
  • To detect, prevent, investigate, and respond to abuse, spam, fraud, security incidents, illegal activity, and violations of our agreements.
  • To provide tools that allow users to report objectionable content and abusive users, block other users, and request review of moderation decisions.
  • To comply with applicable laws, legal requests, regulatory duties, and platform requirements.

3. User-Generated Content Safety

Siney may allow users to publish, share, or interact with user-generated content. We maintain safety systems intended to reduce exposure to objectionable, abusive, illegal, or harmful content.

  • Users can report content or accounts that they believe are abusive, offensive, illegal, unsafe, or otherwise violate our rules.
  • Users can block abusive users where the feature is available.
  • We may review reports, remove or restrict content, limit distribution, suspend accounts, terminate accounts, or notify authorities where appropriate.
  • We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse material, sexual exploitation of minors, credible threats, targeted harassment, hate content, non-consensual intimate content, and content that promotes self-harm, terrorism, or serious violence.
  • We may use human review, automated tools, metadata, hash matching, behavioral signals, and user reports to identify and respond to unsafe content.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following limited cases:

  • With service providers that help us host, secure, analyze, support, and operate Siney.
  • With other users when you publish content, interact publicly, send messages, or choose to make information visible.
  • With platform providers, security partners, or app stores as needed to operate the app, prevent abuse, process reports, or comply with platform rules.
  • With law enforcement, regulators, courts, or other parties when required by law or when necessary to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, restructuring, or transfer of assets, subject to appropriate safeguards.

5. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, maintain safety records, prevent repeat abuse, and protect the service. Deleted content or accounts may remain in backups, logs, moderation records, or legal preservation systems for a limited period where necessary.

6. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, export, restrict, or object to certain processing of your personal information. You may also request account deletion through the app or by contacting support.

7. Children and Minors

Siney is not intended for children under the minimum age required by applicable law. Users who are not old enough to consent to data processing must use Siney only with appropriate parental or guardian consent where permitted. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law. If we learn that such information has been collected, we will take appropriate steps to delete or restrict it.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical, administrative, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International Transfers
